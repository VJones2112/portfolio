About Verónica

Hi friends! I am a former teacher turned web developer. I taught middle school math for 11 years but I knew it was time for me to leave that profession around year 9. My heart was in it but my stress could not be controlled.

When Covid sent us all home starting mid-March 2020, I started coding relentlessly. I had done a couple of Codecademy lessons previously and really enjoyed it, but teaching does not leave time for personal pursuits. I took the change of pace Covid gave us as an opportunity to dig in and started coding daily using Codecademy, freecodecamp, Udemy Courses, the Odin Project, and YouTube tutorials.

When I'm not trying to figure out why my code doesn't work (or does!), I enjoy making things, from spreadsheets to quilts.
